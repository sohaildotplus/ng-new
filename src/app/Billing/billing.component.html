How it works - 3 easy steps

01

Pricing and Tax Calculation:

The "Create Invoice" feature automatically calculates the total cost of services or products, factoring in applicable taxes or discounts. This helps to minimize errors and saves time on manual calculations..

02

Multiple Payment Options:

The "Create Invoice" feature supports various payment methods, including cash, card payments, digital wallets, or salon-specific payment systems. This flexibility allows salon owners to accommodate their clients' preferred payment methods.

03

Online Payments:

Integration with popular payment gateways allows customers to make secure payments directly within the app. This can include options for credit/debit card payments, digital wallets, or even salon-specific memberships.
